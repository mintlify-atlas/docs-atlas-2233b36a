Athena transforms decision-making by treating your **decision history as a compounding asset**. Every choice you make, every tradeoff you evaluate, every risk you accept or reject — all of it becomes context for future decisions.

## Strategic Decision Workflows

### Risk Analysis

<CodeGroup>
```text Session 1: Establish Risk Tolerance
You: "I'm evaluating a business opportunity.
      How do I think about risk?"

AI: "Let's establish your baseline:
     - Financial runway: How many months can you operate
       without income?
     - Dependents: Who relies on your income?
     - Downside tolerance: What's the worst acceptable outcome?"

You: "12 months runway, 2 kids, can't lose more than $50k."

AI: [Files risk profile]
    Risk tolerance: Moderate
    Max loss: $50k
    Time horizon: 12 months
```

```text Session 30: Apply Risk Framework
You: "Evaluating a $40k investment in equipment.
      Expected ROI: 18 months."

AI: [Loads risk profile from Session 1]
    
    Risk check:
    - Investment: $40k (under $50k max loss ✓)
    - Payback: 18 months (beyond 12-month horizon ✗)
    - Runway impact: Reduces to 8 months (risky ✗)
    
    Red flag: Payback period exceeds your risk horizon.
               This violates your Session 1 constraints.
    
    Recommendation: Decline or negotiate shorter payback.
```
</CodeGroup>

### Scenario Analysis

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Scenarios (Session 1)">
    ```text
    You: "Evaluating a career change. Model 3 scenarios:
          1. Stay at current job (baseline)
          2. Take startup offer (risky)
          3. Freelance (flexible)"
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Score Each Scenario (Sessions 2-5)">
    ```text
    Session 2: Score Scenario 1 (current job)
    - Stability: 9/10
    - Growth: 4/10
    - Income: 8/10
    - Fulfillment: 5/10
    
    Session 3: Score Scenario 2 (startup)
    Session 4: Score Scenario 3 (freelance)
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Weight by Your Priorities (Session 6)">
    ```text
    AI: "Based on past decisions, you weight:
         - Stability: 40% (high priority)
         - Growth: 20%
         - Income: 25%
         - Fulfillment: 15%"
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compute Weighted Scores (Session 7)">
    ```text
    AI: Weighted scores:
        1. Current job: 7.35
        2. Startup: 5.80
        3. Freelance: 6.50
    
        Recommendation: Stay at current job.
                        Freelance is second if you value
                        flexibility over income.
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**The AI learns your priority weights** from past decisions. You don't manually score — it infers from your history.
</Tip>

## Practical Tips

<Warning>
**Don't Fake Consistency**

Your risk tolerance, priorities, and goals will evolve. Tell the AI when they change:

"I used to prioritize stability, but I'm ready for more risk now."

The AI updates your profile and flags the shift.
</Warning>

<Tip>
**Use Real Numbers**

Vague: "I want to save more."

Specific: "I want to save $1,000/month for 12 months."

The AI can track progress and hold you accountable.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**File Decisions, Not Just Outcomes**

After every major decision:

```text
You: "I chose Option A because X, Y, Z.
      My alternatives were B and C.
      I rejected B because..."
```

The AI files the *reasoning*, not just the choice.
</Tip>
